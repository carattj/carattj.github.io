Description

I was hired by the SUPSI Computer Forensics Department as Cyber Intelligence Assistant with Bachelor.

The SUPSI university assigned me to an external project in collaboration with Memento Labs, a company that develops and offers numerous solutions in the fight against cybercrime. The project is funded directly by the Swiss Confederation, through the Innosuisse office.

The project consisted of a cyber intelligence framework to monitor and analyze TOR hidden services for improving dark-web investigations.

I worked from home and in person as part of a team in a Agile Scrum work environment. The project was structured following a microservices architecture, therefore I learned how to split wider functionalities into smaller components, and to develop them as as single microservices.

the project was presented at various cybersecurity fairs, including the most local Swiss Cyber Days, and one of the most famous and renowned in the world ISS Middle East in Dubai.
