About me

Hi, I'm Jacopo, I was born in 1999, a breath of the twentieth century and then running away into the twenty-first century. A very technological century, but that's okay with me.

My passion for new technologies began as a child and, and after high school, led me to choose the Computer Science faculty at USI university. I decided for USI in Lugano on the basis of information that confirmed to me how avant-garde the young faculty was. And, in fact, I have not regretted it. Indeed from direct experience, now I know there is hard work to be done and that no one gives anything away, and that's okay with me.

The internships, jobs and projects in which I participated allowed me to understand how vast the IT world is: you never stop learning!

But I also have other passions: music. As a self-taught 'I pinch' the guitar that gives me a lot, I play, sing and compose. I also love practicing fitness and cycling.
